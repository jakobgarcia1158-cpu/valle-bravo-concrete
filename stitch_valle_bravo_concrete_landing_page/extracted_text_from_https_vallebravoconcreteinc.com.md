## Culverts

Culverts are structures make of cement, piping and wood which are designed to provie a pathway through a solid existing or soon to be built structure. An example would be when a water way crosses a road and needs to be channeled in order to prevent damage and wear to the road, as well as move the water away from the road. Culverts are used in both commercial and residential situations and are just a way that builders can make structures work with both natural and unnaturally occuring waterways.
